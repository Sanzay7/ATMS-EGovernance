Welome to Airline Traffic Management System

An E-Governance Platform

Airline Traffic Management System (ATMS) is a sophisticated and comprehensive set of technologies, procedures, and strategies employed to efficiently manage and optimize air traffic in the aviation industry. It plays a crucial role in ensuring the safe, orderly, and expeditious movement of aircraft through the skies, as well as on the ground at airports. The primary objective of ATMS is to enhance airspace capacity, minimize delays, and reduce fuel consumption, thus improving overall flight efficiency and passenger experience.

Basically this e-governance platform is based on critical flow model. This website provides some pilot/co-pilot details and an air traffic controller services to its target audience.


Objectives

Airline Traffic Management System(ATMS) was initiated to meet the following key objectives:

1. Create e-infrastructure which facilitate and promote e-governance.
2. Safe cyberspace.
